🌍 Our Vision
Namenayou™ is an Africa-born innovation platform that connects learning, logistics, and investment into one ecosystem.
By combining education, employment, and logistics financing, Namenayou™ empowers people and businesses to grow together — transforming Africa’s logistics landscape through technology, transparency, and opportunity.

🚛 The Opportunity
$344 Billion logistics market in Sub-Saharan Africa (World Bank, 2023).
7.5% annual growth projected in transport and fleet investments.
Expanding routes: Lagos – Kano | Calabar – Abuja | Nigeria → Ghana → Côte d’Ivoire → Togo.

💡 Our Solution
Namenayou™ Platform Components:
1.Job Board – Connects logistics operators and skilled workers.
2.Learning Hub – Students learn and earn through certified programs.
3.Investor Channel – Enables local & international investors to finance or donate trucks for ROI.
4.Bank Partnerships – Collaboration with Zenith Bank and GTBank ensures secure and traceable transactions.
