🚛 Namenayou — Smart Logistics Investment Ecosystem (HQ: Abuja, Nigeria 🇳🇬)
🌍 Core Mission
To empower Nigerians and global investors to own, donate, or fund durable trucks for Nigeria’s logistics network — generating returns on investment (ROI) while supporting national transportation infrastructure.

💰 1. Investor Participation Models
Namenayou will allow investors — local and international — to pledge investments or donate trucks through flexible partnership models:
🅰️ Truck Purchase & Lease Model
Investor purchases a new truck (China-made or Mercedes) through Namenayou’s verified dealer network.
Namenayou leases it to a verified logistics operator under a managed fleet agreement.
Operator pays monthly lease fees, generating ROI for the investor (8–12% per month depending on utilization).
✅ Benefits:
Asset ownership remains with the investor.
Namenayou manages operations, insurance, maintenance, and driver tracking.
Safe and transparent income stream.

🅱️ Loan & Profit-Sharing Model
Investors provide capital (in ₦ or foreign currency) to fund operational fleets.
Namenayou deploys the funds to vetted operators, using smart contracts for accountability.
Monthly profits are shared — typically 70% (operator) / 30% (investor).
✅ Benefits:
Enables smaller investors to participate (₦500k+).
Transparent financial reports and route analytics.

🅲️ Truck Donation Model (CSR / Development Impact)
Philanthropic investors or organizations donate trucks to Namenayou for national logistics use.
The trucks are leased to Nigerian SMEs or youth operators under low-interest terms.
Donors receive impact recognition (CSR badge, social impact report, and naming rights for trucks).
✅ Benefits:
Supports youth employment and logistics innovation.
Strengthens Nigeria’s transport infrastructure.

Logistics firms and fleet owners can hire verified candidates directly from the Namenayou workforce pool.

🧩 Features

oTruck Drivers (long-distance, local)
oFleet Maintenance Technicians
oLogistics Coordinators
oDispatch Managers
oFinance/Admin Staff

Applicants submit CVs, credentials, and experience summaries.

🔹 Qualification & Verification
Integration with:
oFRSC (for driver’s license verification)
