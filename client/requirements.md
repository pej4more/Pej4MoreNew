## Packages
framer-motion | Page transitions and scroll-triggered animations
lucide-react | Beautiful, consistent icons across the platform
react-hook-form | Form state management
@hookform/resolvers | Zod validation integration for forms

## Notes
- `lucide-react` is used extensively for iconography representing logistics and learning.
- `framer-motion` handles page entry and scroll animations to give the site a premium feel.
- The waitlist form uses standard React Hook Form with Zod validation against the shared API schema.
- Uses standard Wouter for routing.
- The layout is composed of a fixed Navbar and Footer on every page.
